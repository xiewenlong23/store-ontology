# Vertex
**Vertex** 允许您在整个现实世界组织的数字孪生中可视化和量化因果关系。使用 Vertex 工具包,您可以访问和探索由您的组织策展和发布的现有图,或构建新的系统图。

**Vertex** allows you to visualize and quantify cause and effect across the digital twin of your real-world organization. Using the Vertex toolkit, you can access and explore existing graphs that have been curated and published by your organization or build new system graphs.
基于当前、预测或拟议条件，为数据驱动的决策提供依据，Vertex 提供了一套工具包，用于监控、模拟和优化运营决策，以最大化组织成果。

Informing data-driven decisions based on current, predicted, or proposed conditions, Vertex provides a toolkit to monitor, simulate, and optimize operational decision-making to maximize organizational outcomes.
Vertex 可以帮助您：

Vertex can help you:
* **提高透明度**，通过探索跨职能孤岛的整个网络的实时视图。
* **聚焦重要事项**，通过集中告警识别主要风险和机会，以及它们如何影响目标。
* **模拟未来**，结合分析和运营，找出您可以做出的最具影响力的改变。
* **学习与优化**，通过提升数据清晰度和模型准确性，了解哪些有效，并朝着全局最优迈进。

* **Bring transparency** by exploring a live view of your whole network across functional silos.
* **Focus on what's important** with central alerting for top risks and opportunities and how they influence targets.
* **Simulate the future** by combining analytics and operations to find the most impactful changes you can make.
* **Learn and optimize** by improving data clarity and model accuracy to see what's working and work towards a global optimum.
